## Description
Social Media apps are built to allow people to connect and communicate online. They provide a platform to allow people to express their thoughts and feelings. That's why, you guessed it we built one! Our social media app called, Social Site allows users to create your own profile, post your thoughts and have the ability to view others profiles to see what they've posted! This is the **frontend** portion of Social Site. Follow backend guidlines to initiate application by clicking [here](https://github.com/CasssieGithub/Social-Site-Backend). We hope you enjoy our site!
